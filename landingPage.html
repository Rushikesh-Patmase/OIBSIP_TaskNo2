Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni (born 7 July 1981), commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer. Widely considered one of the greatest captain and Wicket-keeper-batsman in history of cricket as well as a best finisher. He was captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014, who plays right-handed wicket-keeper-batsman, known for his calm captaincy and his ability to finish matches in tight situations. He is also the current captain of Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League.
